Our Services

Rice

We are the exporter of Non basmati rice, basmati rice, brown rice and broken rice etc.

We deal with Africa and China for non-basmati and broken rice. In Europe and Saudi Arabia, we supply brown rice and basmati rice.

Agri-Products

Groundnut, cumin, sesame seed, and DOC cattle feed are directly exported to Vietnam, China, Bangladesh, Indonesia, and Korea.

We import Mutter cheek pea, Masoor Dal etc. from Russia, Canada, Ukraine and Africa.

Salt

We export Sea Salt/Raw Salt, Crystal Salt, Iodised, Double Refined & Triple Refined Salt.

Countries served: Indonesia, Africa, Sri Lanka, Bangladesh.

Scrap

We deal in Copper, Aluminium, Brass, Iron, Steel & Metal Waste.

Scrap imported majorly from UK & Africa.

We Mine. We Supply. We Partner.

With over three decades of experience in the coal mining and port operations industry, Maritime Coal Industries has established itself as a leader in sustainable resource extraction and efficient logistics management.

Our commitment to environmental stewardship drives us to continuously innovate and implement cleaner technologies, making us a responsible partner in the global energy supply chain.
